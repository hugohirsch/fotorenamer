Fotorenamer help page



Perform the following steps to use the application:

  1. Create a backup of your pictures!
  2. Launch the application
  3. Choose the directory that contains your pictures (you may want to use the backup directory) - you may add it directly in the textfield or drag'n'drop it onto the textfield
  4. Click the button

    "Start"

    in order to perform the renaming.

What's the application purpose?

Normally digital camera's picture names are:

IMG_number.jpg

Unfortunately you do not get a clue on when the picture was taken. Many camera applications change the date of the image to the date it was written on your hard disc. Therefore the application extracts picture EXIF data and writes the original creation date as a new file name.
The relevant naming schema is:

YYYYMMDD_HHMM_IMG_number.jpg

A positive side effect of ISO-date formats is its naturally sortable by creation date.
EXAMPLE

Your picture

IMG_1865.jpg

will become

20031020_1417_IMG_1865.jpg

if your picture was taken on 2003-10-20 at 2:17 pm.
